# User Profiles

Back to [[Conn - Overview]]

---

## What's on a Profile

- **Name & photo**
- **Bio** - Short intro
- **Genres** - Interests they follow
- **Groups** - [[Conn - Groups & Genres|Groups]] they belong to
- **Top posts** - Their most starred [[Conn - Posts & Accomplishments|accomplishments]]
- **Meetup count** - How many [[Conn - Meetups|meetups]] attended
- **Stars earned** - Total recognition from the community

---

## Profile Visibility

- Public info: name, bio, genres, groups
- Accomplishments visible to group members
- Location shown at city level (not exact)

---

## Ideas
- Profile badges (e.g., "Founding Member", "10 Meetups", "100 Stars")
- Portfolio/link section
- Endorsements from other members
