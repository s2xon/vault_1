## Core Concept

Conn connects people through **genre-based groups** where members can meet up in person or online. Groups are built around specific interests, and members share accomplishments, earn stars, and build a real community.

## High-Level Features

- **Genre-based groups** - Music, fitness, tech, art, gaming, etc.
- **In-person & online meetups** - Flexible meeting formats
- **Accomplishment posts** - Share wins with your group
- **Stars & recognition** - Community-driven recognition system
- **Discovery** - Find groups and people near you
