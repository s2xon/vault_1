# Groups & Genres

Back to [[Conn - Overview]]

---

## How Groups Work

A **group** is a community of people organized around a specific interest. Every group belongs to a **genre** (category).

### Genre Examples

| Genre | Example Groups |
|-------|---------------|
| Fitness | Morning Runners, Powerlifting Crew, Yoga Flow |
| Music | Jazz Nights, Bedroom Producers, Guitar Circle |
| Tech | Indie Hackers, AI Study Group, Open Source Club |
| Art | Figure Drawing, Street Photography, Ceramics Lab |
| Gaming | Smash Locals, DnD Campaign, Speedrun Society |
| Food | Home Chefs, Coffee Snobs, Fermentation Club |

---

## Group Structure

- **Name** - Group identity
- **Genre** - The category it falls under
- **Description** - What the group is about
- **Members** - People in the group
- **Posts** - Shared content and [[Conn - Posts & Accomplishments|accomplishments]]
- **Meetups** - Scheduled [[Conn - Meetups|in-person or online meetups]]
- **Stars** - Total recognition earned by the group

---

## Group Creation

Anyone can create a group. Creator becomes the admin.

1. Pick a genre
2. Name the group
3. Add a description
4. Set location (city/region) or mark as online-only
5. Invite people or make it discoverable
