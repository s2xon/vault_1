# MECOP — Multiple Engineering Cooperative Program

Part of [[Finance]]

---

## What Is MECOP

MECOP is a co-op program through Oregon State University. Students alternate between school terms and full-time paid work terms at engineering companies. Typically 3–5 work terms total, each lasting 6 months.

Companies that participate: Intel, HP Inc., Hewlett Packard Enterprise, Tektronix, Daimler Trucks, FLIR, and others.

- [ ] Network with engineers at the company
- [ ] Figure out which domain I want to go deeper in
